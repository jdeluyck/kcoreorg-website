In another episode of totally wonky legal battles, [eBay](https://www.ebay.com/) was [found guilty](http://web.archive.org/web/20080606223245/http://afp.google.com:80/article/ALeqM5ieVhlM9xNEzcmDtrdw6ToFLtkRAg)[^ia1] of selling counterfeit [Hermes](http://www.hermes.com/) handbags by a french court.

So, let me get this straight:

* Person A sells something on eBay that's a fake
* Person B buys said fake
* Person B finds out it's a fake, and instead of taking it up with the seller attacks the company that's a mere falicitator of online auctions...
* ... and the french courts follow him?!??
Me no comprendo.
