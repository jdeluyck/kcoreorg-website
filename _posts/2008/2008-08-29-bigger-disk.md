Upgraded my Macbook with a bigger disk: from an 80gb [Toshiba](https://web.archive.org/web/20070607165320/https://web.archive.org/web/20070607165320/http://www.storage.toshiba.eu:80/)[^ia1] [MK8034GSX](https://hddfaqs.com/toshiba-mk8034gsx/)[^hddfaqs1] drive (with which it came delivered) to a (secondhand) 320gb [Hitachi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HGST) [HTS543232L9A300](https://hddfaqs.com/hitachi-hts543232l9a300/)[^hddfaqs2] (what's in a name...). Long live diskspace! ;)

And thanks to [Carbon Copy Cloner](https://bombich.com/) the migration was painless.
