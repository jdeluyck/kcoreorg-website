I recently acquired a [Mac Mini](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mac_Mini#Intel_polycarbonate_(2006%E2%80%932009)), which I'm going to use as a [PVR](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PVR).

Together with an [Elgato](https://www.elgato.com/eu/en) [EyeTV Hybrid](http://web.archive.org/web/20080915201856/http://www.elgato.com:80/elgato/na/mainmenu/products/hybrid.en.html)[^ia1] it works perfectly, attached to my good old analogue 21" Sony TV .

Sofar it seems to work fine, using a DVI-to-svideo convertor. I've installed the EyeTV software, the [PyeTV](https://code.google.com/archive/p/pyetv) [Front Row](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Front_Row) plugin (which allows me to controle EyeTV from within Front Row) and [Perian](https://perian.org/) (for more codec support in Front Row)

More on this can be found on the [Hicksdesign](https://hicks.design/journal/mac-mini-media-centre) blog ;)
