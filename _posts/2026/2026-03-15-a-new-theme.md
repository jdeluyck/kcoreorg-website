My static blog to-do list kept growing, so I decided to do something about it.

I've switched from [Minimal Mistakes](https://github.com/mmistakes/minimal-mistakes/) to [Chirpy](https://chirpy.cotes.page/) as a theme, because it offers built-in dark/light theme support and has share-to-Mastodon functionality. At the same time I've done some spring cleaning in my posts - moved some really old stuff off to the side, and generally made it look a lot prettier.

I'm happy with the end result. If you're reading this via RSS, you shouldn't notice much ;)
