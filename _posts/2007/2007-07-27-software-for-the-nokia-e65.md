After owning my Nokia E65 for about a month, here's the list of apps I installed on it:

* [Conversation](http://web.archive.org/web/20071011021726/http://www.nokia.com:80/A4568203)[^ia15]
* [Device Status](https://web.archive.org/web/20071011035054/http://www.nokia.com:80/A4596393)[^ia1]
* ~~Fring~~
* [Gmail](http://web.archive.org/web/20070808152532/http://www.google.com:80/mobile/mail/index.html)[^ia2]
* [Google Maps](http://web.archive.org/web/20070808073053/http://www.google.com:80/gmm/index.html)[^ia6]
* [Handy Alarm](https://web.archive.org/web/20061029222616/http://nokia-e61-software.epocware.com:80/Handy_Alarm.html)[^ia7]
* [Handy Keylock](http://web.archive.org/web/20070721052045/http://nokia-e61-software.epocware.com/Handy_Keylock.html)[^ia8]
* [Handy Profiles](http://web.archive.org/web/20070812000114/http://nokia-e61-software.epocware.com:80/Handy_Profiles.html)[^ia9]
* [Handy Taskman](https://web.archive.org/web/20061031091738/http://nokia-e61-software.epocware.com:80/Handy_Taskman.html)[^ia10]
* [Mail for Exchange](http://web.archive.org/web/20070716014255/http://www.businesssoftware.nokia.com:80/mail_for_exchange_downloads.php)[^ia11]
* [Opera Mobile](http://web.archive.org/web/20070806024310/http://www.opera.com:80/products/mobile/products/s60/)[^ia3]
* ~~Opera Mini~~
* [PhoNetInfo](https://www.patrickfrei.ch/phonetinfo/)
* [Putty](https://s2putty.sourceforge.net/)
* [Reflect Theme](/2007/07/27/reflect-theme-for-symbian/)
* [Screenshot](https://www.antonypranata.com/screenshot/)
* [TextQuick](http://web.archive.org/web/20070824224429/http://mind-flip.com:80/textquick/index.html)[^ia12]
* [TomTom](https://www.tomtom.com/)
* [Truphone](https://www.truphone.com/)
* [Y-Browser](https://web.archive.org/web/20070714023043/http://www.drjukka.com:80/YBrowser.html)[^ia13] (+ plugins)
* [RemindMe](http://www.mobifunsoft.com/RemindMe.html)

You can get the Handy Alarm, Handy Keylock and Handy Taskman as the [Handy Essentials](https://web.archive.org/web/20061021020415/http://nokia-e61-software.epocware.com:80/Handy_Essentials.html)[^ia14] pack.

And ofcourse, some games:

* [Frozen Bubble](https://web.archive.org/web/20060101031900/http://fb-s60.sourceforge.net:80/?)[^ia4]
* [MiniBlaster](http://www.martin.st/software/blaster/)
* Rollingball
* Snakes 3D
* [S-Tris 2](https://web.archive.org/web/20061008224235/http://www.elementsgames.com:80/stris2/)[^ia5]
