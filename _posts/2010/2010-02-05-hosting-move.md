I've moved hosting, from [Lunar Pages](https://web.archive.org/web/20060101014422/http://lunarpages.com:80/)[^ia1] to [OVH](https://www.ovhcloud.com/en-gb/). Lunar Pages is a good hosting, but their [overselling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Overselling#Web_Hosting) and price hikes in the last few years made me look for something else.

Other reasons:

* speed: transfers are a LOT faster (especially in peak hours)
* SSH access ;)
* Full DNS zone access
* Cheaper prices for domain names
* ...

If you encounter any oddities, feel free to let me know...
